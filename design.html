Design

You will see graphic design that ranges over a period of 30 years.
Clients included TAFE Colleges, IBM in association with Milford Industries, Interactive Imagery, Titania Prostheses, Yankalilla Regional News and many others. I have also lectured at The Technology School of the Future, where I was involved with teaching early animation software.

I also gained seven years of experience as a Graphic Designer and Draftsperson through employment with The South Australian Lands Titles Office and Department of Environment and Planning. Some of my responsibilities included the annual report, large format sign design, brochures and illustrations for resource material and drafting.
